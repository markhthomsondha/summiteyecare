About Our Practice

Welcome to Summit Eye Care

For over a decade, Dr. Vic Khemsara and our team of eye care professionals have been serving patients of all ages with kindness and compassion in Winston-Salem, NC.

We provide the most advanced examination procedures, therapies, and surgical procedures. We are committed to creating an exceptional experience for our patients, treating them as family members whose health and well-being is our highest priority.

Whether your visit is for routine vision tests or treatment of conditions like cataracts, dry eye, and glaucoma, we'll take the time to fully explain your diagnosis and treatment plan, answering all of your questions to your satisfaction.
